Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is the process for buying a car?
Our car buying process is simple and straightforward. First, browse our inventory online or visit our showroom. Once you've selected a car, you can take it for a test drive. If you decide to purchase, we'll help you with financing options and complete the paperwork. Finally, you can drive away in your new car!
2. Do you offer financing options?
Yes, we offer a variety of financing options to suit different budgets and credit profiles. Our finance team will work with you to find the best solution for your needs.
3. Can I trade in my old car?
Absolutely! We accept trade-ins and will offer you a competitive price based on the market value of your vehicle. The value of your trade-in can be applied towards the purchase of your new car.
4. Do you provide warranties on your cars?
Yes, we offer warranties on most of our cars. The specifics of the warranty will depend on the make and model of the car. Our sales team can provide you with detailed information about the warranty options available for the car you're interested in.
5. How can I schedule a test drive?
You can schedule a test drive by contacting us via phone or email, or by using the form on our website. We'll arrange a convenient time for you to come in and take the car for a spin.
6. What documents do I need to buy a car?
You'll need a valid driver's license, proof of insurance, and proof of income or financing approval. Additional documents may be required depending on your financing arrangement.
7. Do you offer extended warranties?
Yes, we offer extended warranties for added peace of mind. Our team can provide you with the details and help you choose the best option for your needs.
8. Can I buy a car online?
Yes, you can complete most of the car buying process online, including browsing our inventory, applying for financing, and scheduling a test drive. Final paperwork can be completed in person or delivered to you.
9. Do you offer car insurance?
While we don't offer car insurance directly, we can refer you to our trusted insurance partners who can help you find the best coverage for your new car.
10. Can I return a car after purchase?
Our return policy varies depending on the specific circumstances of the purchase. Please speak with our sales team to understand the options available to you.
11. How do I know if a car is still available?
Our online inventory is updated regularly, but availability can change quickly. We recommend contacting us directly to confirm the availability of a specific car.
12. Do you sell certified pre-owned cars?
Yes, we offer a selection of certified pre-owned cars that have been thoroughly inspected and come with a warranty for added peace of mind.
13. What if I have bad credit?
We work with a variety of lenders to offer financing options for all credit situations. Our finance team will work with you to find a solution that fits your needs.
14. Do you offer maintenance and repair services?
Yes, we have a full-service maintenance and repair department staffed by certified technicians. We offer a range of services to keep your car running smoothly.
15. Can I customize my new car?
Yes, we offer a variety of customization options, including aftermarket parts and accessories. Our team can help you personalize your car to suit your style and needs.
16. What are your business hours?
Our business hours are Monday to Friday from 9 AM to 7 PM, Saturday from 10 AM to 5 PM, and closed on Sundays. Please check our website for any holiday hours or special events.
17. Do you offer delivery services?
Yes, we offer delivery services for your convenience. Please speak with our sales team to arrange delivery and discuss any associated costs.
18. How can I contact customer service?
You can contact our customer service team by phone, email, or through the contact form on our website. We're here to help with any questions or concerns you may have.
19. Do you offer any discounts or promotions?
We frequently offer discounts and promotions on our cars and services. Check our website or contact our sales team to learn about current offers and how you can save.
20. Can I test drive multiple cars?
Yes, you can test drive multiple cars to help you make an informed decision. Simply let our sales team know which cars you're interested in, and we'll arrange the test drives for you.
